# CSC-175-W2-Roulette
CSC-175-W2 Final Project for Group 6

1 - Roulette Wheel
0-36 (18 red, 18 black) + 1 "00 pocket"
Bet on a number, group of numbers, or a color represented on a table (3x12)
Players are rewarded based on what gets rolled compared to what they bet.

2 - Competition
Run a second roll and randomize a bet.
Compare with player score to determine "win" condition.
